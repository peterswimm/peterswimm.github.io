Present Rockem Sockem @ Otto’s Shrunken Head 538 East 14th Street, between Avenue A & B Every Second Friday of the Month. 10-4 am Free 21+ Big night, big fun. Spring is in the air and were getting fiesty. April Rockem Sockem we have Guest DJ Allan Diaz from the band P.I.L. djing at 1 pm and tag teaming with us after.
