"New Arduinoboy from XIWI Electronics"

Peter L. Swimm
2013-06-23
Music Hardware › Reviews › Conversational AI › Music › Tutorials
arduinoboy, chiptune, gameboy, hardware, live-performance, lsdj, midi, review, xiwi-electronics

For the last few years I have been using the Arduinoboy MIDI output feature in my sets to control my external MIDI gear with my Nintendo Game Boy running LSDJ. This has allowed me to focus on crowd control and trying to remember the three chords each song has with some decent results.

For most of that time I have been using a Scienceguy built unit, but I have never been happy about its build quality and worried about the day when the unit finally bites the dust. Recently I saw a new product from Brooklyn based tinkerer XIWI Electronics which looked pretty robust and I figured what the hell, and ordered a unit from their online shop.

Build Quality

The unit itself is solidly built. It has sleek looks and the detachable link cable and power supply are handy in connecting to my various Game Boy units. One thing I noticed is that some modded Game Boys are no longer able to power the Scienceguy via the link cable, so having external power is something I will have to research to see if it has any impact on solving the issue.

The unit is powered using my Voodoo Labs pedal power unit that I use to power my Nord Micro Modular, my Shruthi-1, and various other gear with no complaints. The push button looks gorgeous and feels great, but in operation it is a little finicky and may require double presses to select the various modes.

  • I use four channels of MIDI

  • The whole range of the CC is scaled across the usable range of the CC message
  • The noise channel is mapped to channel 3 which controls all my various effects boxes

Conclusion

This is a very robust, high value unit that simply works as advertised. The unit is sturdy and reliable in limited testing and is a good product for those who are looking for getting into LSDJ syncing and external MIDI control. If anyone has questions please post them in the comments.
