01/21/12 MonkeyWrench Books Benefit Show with RainbowDragonEyes & ATX Noise

Peter Swimm
2012-01-11
Live Shows
I Cactus, RainbowDragonEyes, Yatagarasu, Yoshi Eat Da Mawio

Saturday the 21st, come get an earful of all the square waves you can possibly handle at MonkeyWrench books! Peter Swimm - Austin, TX Yatagarasu — Austin, TX I, Cactus — Austin, TX Yoshi Eat Da Mawio — Austin, TX When Saturday, January 21, 2012 8:00pm until 11:30pm MonkeyWrench Books 110 E North Loop, Austin, TX 78751 RSVP
