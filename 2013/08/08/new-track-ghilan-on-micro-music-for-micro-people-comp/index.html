New Track: 'Ghilan' on 'Micro Music For Micro People' Comp

Peter L. Swimm
2013-08-08
Music › Releases
Calmdownkidder Records

About forty years ago I submitted a track to CDK’s “Micro Music For Micro People” compilation and now it’s finally been released!

The comp is “a chipmusic tribute to Fat Wreck Chord’s Small Music For Small People. 101 tracks, 101 different artists, all approximately 30 seconds or less”. Aside from my track there are ones from some of the most talented artists in the field, many of whom I am privileged to call friends. You can hear my track, and throw CDK some money at Bandcamp.
